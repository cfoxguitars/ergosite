Ordering

Contact us to discuss the model, options, and custom features, after which we'll send an order confirmation document to you specifying what we have agreed to, along with an estimated completion date. When you sign this order confirmation and return it along with your deposit, your order will be booked and its slot in the building schedule secured.

A deposit of one-third of the instrument's total cost is required when you place your order. A second one-third payment is due when work begins on your guitar. The final one-third payment is due when your instrument is completed, prior to shipment. Prices are held firm from the receipt of your deposit until delivery.
