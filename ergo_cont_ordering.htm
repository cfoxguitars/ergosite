Ordering

Contact us to discuss the model, options, and custom features that are best for you. We'll then send you an order confirmation itemizing the details of your instrument and its projected completion date. When you sign and return this order confirmation along with your deposit, your order will be booked and its position in the building schedule secured.

An initial deposit of 1/3 of the instrument's total cost is required to place your order. A second 1/3 payment is due when work begins on your guitar, usually 4-5 months before delivery. The final 1/3 payment is due when your instrument is completed, ready to ship.
