Features


Low-mass parabolic soundboard

The ergo's most revolutionary feature is its ultra low-mass parabolic sound board *. This composite plate - two thin skins of master-grade tone wood sandwiching a 1/16" core of high-tech honey-comb material - weighs 40% less than its solid wood equivalent; less weight to sap the strings' energy, less weight to damp their finer harmonic content. The sound board's spherical contour and 'torsion box' structure creates tremendous strength and stability without the heavy X braces that support conventional steel string sound boards, for a further dramatic weight reduction. The ergo's acoustic power and character is due largely to the high strength-to-weight ratio of this remarkable sound board.
