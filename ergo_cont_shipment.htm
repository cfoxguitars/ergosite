Shipment & Warranty

Your guitar will be shipped to you by Federal Express. If upon receipt of your instrument you are not completely satisfied, you may contact us within 48 hours to express any concerns. If you wish to return the instrument, a full refund will be made upon the safe return and subsequent resale of the undamaged instrument.

Charles Fox guitars are covered by a limited warranty to the original purchaser during the working life of the builder against defective materials or workmanship. Upon notice of any defect, Charles Fox will repair or replace, at his option, any defective part or material. Instruments returned for warranty repair must be shipped pre-paid and will be returned collect postage. Damage incurred by misuse, accidents, normal wear or natural cracking of wood or finishes due to changes in temperature or humidity is not covered under the warranty. This is the sole warranty of Charles Fox Guitars and includes the warranty of merchantability and fitness for a particular purpose. No other written or oral warranty applies.
