Introduction

Playability

We commonly mistake the limitations of an instrument itself for the limits of our own musicianship. It's a revelation to experience how effortlessly one can play on a true performance quality guitar. To achieve the transparent playability that Charles Fox guitars are known for, an uncommon amount of time and attention is lavished on every aspect of the ergo's neck, bridge and overall setup. The ergo's hand-shaped, mirror polished fret work is unsurpassed. Through subtle adjustments of its neck width, string spread, action and other such details, each ergo is optimised to suit its owner's personal playing style.
