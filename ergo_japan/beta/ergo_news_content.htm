PERFORMANCE

Legendary classical guitarist John Williams (playing a Greg Smallman guitar) and virtuoso jazz guitarist John Etheridge (playing the new Fox ergo) have teamed up to record and tour as a steel string - nylon string duo, featuring new material showcasing the two distinct and complementary voices of these instruments. Their first concert was also the ergo's European debut at London's popular Pizza Express Jazz club. Earlier in the week, a few pieces from the duo's new program aired live on the BBC Radio program InTune.

Upcoming US tour dates include October 19th and 22nd at San Francisco's Herbst Hall, and October 24th at Seattle's Benaroya Hall, sponsored by the Seattle Symphony. A more extensive US tour is scheduled for April, 2007. Check back for additional dates and details.
