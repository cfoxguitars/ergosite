Introduction

Design

The ergo began as a simple plan to adapt the classical guitar's revolutionary 'double-top' sound board technology to the more severe requirements of the steel string guitar. The ergo's remarkable power and acoustic character is the fruit of that project. Building on this strength, the ergo is designed to extend the limits of the steel string instrument in every way and to represent the emerging steel string 'super guitar' at this point in time. The ergo exists to inspire fresh musical ideas and to offer a perfect tool for realizing that inspiration.
