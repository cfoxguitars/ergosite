Models

The ergo noir, with it's striking flitch-matched cocobolo or optional Brazilian rosewood body, jet black face and ebony bindings with black & red pinstripe trim, is a dark beauty with endless attitude and sex appeal.
The ergo blanc, with its flitch-matched deeply figured sapele or flamed maple body, natural spruce face, ebony or cocobolo (or optional snakewood) bindings with black & red pinstripe trim, is as warmly bright and inviting as its noir cousin is darkly seductive.

Each model is available in two body styles:
15 1/8" / 384mm Grand Concert (GC) and 16 1/4" / 413mm Small Jumbo (SJ).
And two neck styles:
12 frets off the body with 21 total frets and 14 frets off the body for 24 total frets.

Model names reference each ergo's basic look, body size and neck choice.
For example, ergo noir SJ-14
